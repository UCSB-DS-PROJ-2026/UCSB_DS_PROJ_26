# SB Housing Intelligence
## Overview
For this project, we address the issue of the expensive housing market in Santa Barbara, especially for students seeking off-campus housing. It is often difficult to find affordable options due to limited availability and rising prices. Cities like Isla Vista and Goleta are highly competitive rental markets with limited housing listings and increasing demand each year, leading to continued price growth.

## Problem Statement:
Students in Santa Barbara face significant challenges in finding affordable and reliable housing due to limited availability, high demand, and a lack of transparency in pricing data. Rental listings often do not provide predictive insights or student-focused information, making it difficult for students to plan financially and secure housing.

## Solution
To address this issue, we are developing a web-based platform that:
• Predicts Future housing prices in Santa Barbara 
• Displays current rental listings and housing trends
• Provides student-focused information such as the number of rooms/bathrooms, rent price, distance from UCSB, and more to help students make informed financial decisions

## Features
• AI Chat Explorer

• Housing price prediction model

• Trends and data visualization

• Red Fin, Zillow, and more housing websites
